About 1st Strike Pest & Termite

At 1st Strike Pest & Termite, we specialize in fast, effective, and family-safe pest solutions across Arizona. From rodents and general pests to weeds, termites, and solar panel bird-proofing, we protect what matters most — your home, your family, and your peace of mind.

With deep local knowledge and years of hands-on experience, our approach is precise and proactive. We don’t believe in one-size-fits-all treatments. Instead, we tailor every job to the property and pest issue at hand — delivering dependable results, every time.

Whether you're looking for preventative service or need a one-time corrective treatment, we’re ready to strike first — so pests don’t get the chance.
